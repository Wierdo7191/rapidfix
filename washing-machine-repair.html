Common Washing Machine Issues We Repair

Washing machines are essential appliances that can develop various mechanical and electrical faults. Our experienced technicians diagnose and fix all types of washing machine problems:

Drum & Mechanical Issues

  • Drum not spinning or making noise
  • Broken drum paddles/baffles
  • Drum bearing failure
  • Door lock mechanism faults

Motor & Electrical Faults

  • Motor not running or overheating
  • Belt or coupling failure
  • PCB control board issues
  • Wiring and connection faults

Water & Drainage Problems

  • Machine not filling with water
  • Water not draining
  • Pump failure or blockage
  • Inlet valve issues

Control & Sensor Issues

  • Program selector not working
  • Temperature sensor faults
  • Water level sensor problems
  • Display or touch panel issues

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my washing machine drum not spinning?

Common causes include a broken belt, faulty motor, worn-out drum bearings, or a malfunctioning door lock. Our technicians will diagnose the exact cause and provide the most cost-effective repair solution.

Why won't my washing machine drain?

Draining issues are usually caused by a clogged drain hose, faulty drain pump, or blocked filter. We'll inspect the entire drainage system and clear any blockages or replace faulty components.

Is it worth repairing an old washing machine?

Washing machines typically last 8-12 years. If your machine is relatively young and the repair cost is less than 50% of a new unit, repair is usually worthwhile. Our technicians will give you honest advice on whether to repair or replace.

How long does washing machine repair take?

Most common repairs (pump, belt, minor electrical issues) can be completed within 2-4 hours. More complex repairs involving motor or PCB replacement may take 24-48 hours depending on parts availability.
