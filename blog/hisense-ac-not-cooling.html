Hisense Air Conditioner Not Cooling – 5 Quick Fixes

6 min read By RapidFix Kenya Technical Team July 4, 2026

When Nairobi's heat hits 32°C, a Hisense AC that blows warm air becomes an emergency. This issue affects thousands of Kenyan households during the dry season when AC units run continuously.

5 Quick Fixes to Try Immediately

  1. Clean or replace air filters – Clogged filters reduce airflow by 50% in dusty Nairobi conditions
  2. Reset the AC unit – Turn off at the breaker for 10 minutes to reset internal sensors
  3. Check outdoor unit clearance – Ensure 50cm free space around the condenser
  4. Verify thermostat setting – Set to COOL mode, 22-24°C for effective cooling
  5. Inspect remote control batteries – Weak batteries send weak signals causing partial operation

Why Hisense ACs Fail to Cool in Kenya

1. Low Refrigerant Gas (Most Common Issue)

Hisense units lose 10-15% gas annually in Kenya's heat cycles. Low refrigerant means the compressor runs but produces no cooling effect.

2. Dirty Condenser Coils

Outdoor units in Nairobi accumulate red Harmattan dust within weeks. Pressure washing restores efficiency.

3. Faulty Compressor Capacitor

Power surges damage capacitors, preventing the compressor from starting. Listen for a humming sound without cooling.

4. Blocked Air Vents Indoors

Closed or blocked indoor vents force the AC to work harder while delivering minimal cooling.

5. Incorrect Mode Selection

FAN mode circulates air without cooling. Ensure remote shows SNOWFLAKE icon for cooling mode.

People Also Ask: Hisense AC Issues

How much does Hisense AC gas recharge cost in Nairobi?

Average cost ranges KSh 3,000-5,000 for 12,000-18,000 BTU units. Includes gas, labor, and leak detection.

Why does my Hisense AC cool at night but not during day?

High ambient temperature reduces cooling efficiency. Units over 3 years old struggle when outside temps exceed 30°C.

Can a power surge damage my Hisense AC?

Yes. Kenya's voltage fluctuations damage control boards, capacitors, and compressors. Use surge protectors on new installations.

Pro Tip: Set your Hisense AC to DRY mode during humid months. It removes moisture while using 30% less power.
