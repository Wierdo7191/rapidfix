LG Washing Machine Not Spinning – Common Causes and Fixes

7 min read By RapidFix Kenya Technical Team July 4, 2026

When your LG washing machine stops spinning, laundry day grinds to a halt. This is one of the most common issues we see in Kenyan households, especially with older LG front-load models.

Before panicking, run through our systematic diagnosis to determine if it's a simple fix or time for professional repair.

Immediate Checks for Non-Spinning LG Washers

  1. Lid switch safety mechanism – Ensure the door locks properly. If it won't latch, spin is disabled
  2. Unbalanced load detection – LG machines stop spinning with uneven loads to protect the motor
  3. Water drainage status – The washer won't spin if water remains in the drum
  4. Power supply verification – Check for tripped breakers or loose connections

Top 5 Causes of LG Washer Spin Problems in Kenya

1. Faulty Lid Switch Assembly (Most Common)

Nairobi's hard water causes corrosion in electrical contacts. LG's lid switch fails in 60% of spin issues we diagnose. Symptoms include:

  • Door won't lock during cycle
  • Machine fills with water but won't spin
  • Lid lock light flashes repeatedly

2. Broken Drive Belt

Worn belts slip during high-speed spin cycles. They typically last 3-5 years in Kenyan conditions due to heat and humidity.

3. Drain Pump Blockage

Coins, hair, and lint clog the drain pump. LG's error codes OE or IE indicate drainage problems preventing spin.

4. Motor Coupling Wear

The motor coupling connects the motor to the transmission. Hard water minerals accelerate wear in Kenya's water conditions.

5. Shock Absorber Failure

Broken shock absorbers cause excessive vibration during spin. LG washing machines automatically reduce spin speed or stop if vibration exceeds limits.

People Also Ask: LG Washing Machine Issues

Why does my LG washer stop spinning during the cycle?

LG machines have safety sensors that stop spinning for unbalance, drainage issues, or lid problems. Check error codes displayed on the control panel.

How do I reset my LG washing machine after an error?

Turn off the machine, unplug for 5 minutes, hold the spin button for 5 seconds while powering on. The display should clear error codes.

Can I still use my LG washer if spin isn't working?

Yes, use the 'wash only' cycle for delicate items. However, clothes will remain wet and may develop odors. Professional repair recommended within 48 hours.

Kenya-Specific Maintenance Tips

  • Water pressure – LG washers need 0.5-8 bar. Low pressure from rooftop tanks causes spin cycle interruptions
  • Hard water scale – Monthly vinegar cleaning prevents buildup on sensors and valves
  • Power surges – Install a surge protector; voltage spikes damage LG's electronic control boards

Pro Tip: Run an empty hot water cycle monthly to clean the drum and prevent odor buildup from Kenya's humid conditions.
