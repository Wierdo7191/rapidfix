Whirlpool Oven Not Heating? DIY Checks Before Calling a Pro

6 min read By RapidFix Kenya Technical Team July 4, 2026

A Whirlpool oven that won't heat ruins dinner plans and wastes electricity. In Kenya's power-surge environment, heating elements and thermostats fail more frequently than expected.

Safety First: Before Opening Your Oven

Warning: Always disconnect power at the main switch before inspecting heating elements. Internal voltages can cause serious injury.

Quick Visual Checks (Power Off)

  1. Inspect heating elements – Look for broken coils or blistering on the element surface
  2. Check element connections – Loose wires cause intermittent heating
  3. Test oven light operation – If the light works, power reaches the oven
  4. Verify thermostat position – Ensure temperature knob is functioning

Common Heating Failures in Whirlpool Ovens

1. Blown Heating Elements (Most Frequent)

Nairobi's voltage fluctuations cause heating elements to burn out. A functioning element shows 20-50 ohms resistance when tested with a multimeter.

2. Faulty Oven Thermostat

Thermostats stick from heat cycling. Set to 200°C and verify with an oven thermometer if the actual temperature matches.

3. Failed Igniter (Gas Models)

Igniters glow but won't spark. Visible cracks or white powder indicate replacement needed.

People Also Ask: Whirlpool Oven Issues

Why does my Whirlpool oven take forever to heat?

Slow heating indicates a failing element or voltage issues. Test each element separately with a multimeter. Kenya's low voltage (180V-200V) affects heating performance.

Can I still use my oven if bake element is broken?

Yes, if broil element works. Use broil function on lower temperature for baking. However, this stresses remaining components.

How much does oven heating element replacement cost in Kenya?

Whirlpool heating elements range KSh 1,500-2,500 including labor. Parts are readily available through LG Kenya distributors.

Pro Tip: Run a baking soda + water cleaning cycle monthly to remove grease that affects heat distribution.
