Common Microwave Issues We Repair

Microwave ovens can develop various mechanical and electronic faults. Our experienced technicians diagnose and fix all types of microwave problems:

Heating & Magnetron Issues

  • Microwave not heating food
  • Magnetron failure or damage
  • High voltage diode faults

Turntable & Mechanical Issues

  • Turntable not rotating
  • Broken turntable motor
  • Turntable roller damage
  • Door latch or switch faults

Sparking & Safety Issues

  • Sparking inside the microwave
  • Rusted or damaged interior
  • Waveguide cover damage
  • Metallic objects causing sparks

Control Panel & Display

  • Touch panel not responding
  • Display showing errors
  • Button or keypad failures
  • Control board malfunctions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use a microwave that is sparking?

No, sparking inside a microwave indicates a serious safety issue. Common causes include damaged interior coating, faulty stirrer/mode stirrer, or foreign objects inside. Stop using the microwave immediately and contact us for inspection.

Why does my microwave run but not heat?

This is usually caused by a faulty magnetron, high-voltage diode, capacitor, or transformer. These components generate the microwaves that heat food. Our technicians can diagnose and replace the faulty component.

How long does microwave repair take?

Most common repairs (turntable motor, door switches, touch panel) can be completed within 1-2 hours. Magnetron replacement or control board repairs may take 24-48 hours depending on parts availability.

Is microwave repair worth it?

Microwaves typically last 7-10 years. If your microwave is relatively new and the repair cost is reasonable, repair is usually worthwhile. For older models with multiple faults, replacement may be more economical. Our technicians will give you honest advice.
