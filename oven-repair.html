Common Oven & Cooker Issues We Repair

Ovens and cookers are essential kitchen appliances that require expert attention when faults occur. Our experienced technicians diagnose and fix all types of oven and cooker problems:

Heating Issues

  • Oven not heating or heating unevenly
  • Heating element failure or damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use my oven if the heating element is damaged?

No, using an oven with a damaged heating element is dangerous and can cause electrical fires or uneven cooking. If you notice sparks, broken elements, or inconsistent heating, stop using the oven immediately and contact us for repair.

Why does my gas cooker make clicking sounds?

Clicking sounds are normal when igniting gas burners. However, continuous clicking without ignition, or clicking after the burner is lit, indicates a faulty igniter or control valve. This should be repaired promptly as it wastes gas and poses safety risks.

How often should I service my oven?

We recommend professional oven servicing every 12 months. Regular maintenance includes cleaning heating elements, checking gas connections, calibrating thermostats, and inspecting door seals. This ensures safe, efficient operation and extends the appliance's lifespan.

Do you repair gas leaks in cookers?

Yes, our technicians are trained to safely detect and repair gas leaks in cookers. We perform pressure tests, inspect gas valves and hoses, and ensure all connections are secure. Safety is our top priority with all gas appliance repairs.
