Common Home Theatre Issues We Repair

Home theatre systems are complex audio-visual setups that can develop various electronic and connectivity issues. Our experienced technicians diagnose and fix all types of home theatre problems:

Amplifier & Receiver Issues

  • Amplifier not powering on
  • Distorted audio output
  • Channel failures or imbalances
  • Overheating or protection mode

Speaker Problems

  • No sound from specific speakers
  • Speaker cone damage or tearing
  • Crossover network faults
  • Wire connection issues

Connectivity Issues

  • HDMI ARC/eARC problems
  • Optical or coaxial audio faults
  • Bluetooth pairing failures
  • Wireless surround connection issues

Electronic & Control Faults

  • Remote control not working
  • Display or LED errors
  • Firmware update failures
  • Power supply issues

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my home theatre receiver overheating?

Overheating is usually caused by blocked ventilation, dust buildup on internal components, or a failing fan. Our technicians will clean internal components, check ventilation, and replace faulty cooling components if needed.

Why is there no sound from my center speaker?

This could be due to a faulty speaker, damaged crossover, loose wiring, or a channel failure in the receiver. We'll systematically test each component to identify and fix the issue.

Can you fix HDMI ARC issues?

Yes, we diagnose and repair HDMI ARC/eARC connectivity problems between your TV and audio equipment. This includes port repairs, firmware updates, and configuration optimization.

How long does home theatre repair take?

Simple repairs like speaker wiring or remote control issues can be done in 1-2 hours. Amplifier and receiver repairs may take 2-5 days depending on parts availability and complexity.
