Control & Electronic Faults

  • PCB board issues

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my dishwasher not cleaning properly?

Common causes include clogged spray arms, worn or damaged spray nozzles, low water temperature, insufficient detergent, or a faulty circulation pump. Our technicians will identify the exact cause and fix it efficiently.

Why won't my dishwasher drain?

Draining issues are usually caused by a clogged drain hose, faulty drain pump, or full filter. We'll inspect the entire drainage system and clear any blockages or replace faulty components.

Is it worth repairing an old dishwasher?

Dishwashers typically last 8-12 years. If your dishwasher is relatively young and the repair cost is less than 50% of a new unit, repair is usually worthwhile. Our technicians will give you honest advice on whether to repair or replace.

How long does dishwasher repair take?

Most common repairs (spray arms, filters, minor electrical issues) can be completed within 2-4 hours. More complex repairs involving pumps or control boards may take 24-48 hours depending on parts availability.
