Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my AC running but not cooling?

Common causes include low refrigerant levels, dirty condenser coils, faulty compressor, or malfunctioning thermostat. Our technicians will diagnose the exact issue and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

How often should I service my AC?

We recommend professional AC maintenance every 6 months, especially before the hot season. Regular servicing includes cleaning filters and coils, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical connections, and testing overall performance.

My AC is leaking water - is this normal?

Some condensation is normal, but excessive water leakage indicates a problem. Common causes include clogged drain lines, dirty air filters, low refrigerant, or a faulty condensate pump. We'll inspect and fix the issue promptly.

How long does AC repair take?

Simple repairs like filter replacement or electrical fixes can be done in 1-2 hours. More complex repairs involving compressor replacement or gas recharge may take 4-6 hours or require a follow-up visit depending on parts availability.
