Common TV Issues We Repair

Modern televisions are complex devices with delicate components. Our certified technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all types of TV problems efficiently:

Power Issues

  • TV won't turn on or no power indicator
  • Intermittent power or sudden shutdowns
  • Flickering standby light
  • Power supply board failures

Display Problems

  • Cracked or damaged screen panels
  • Dead pixels or vertical/horizontal lines
  • Backlight failure or dim screen
  • Color distortion or image retention

Smart TV Software

  • Apps crashing or not loading
  • Wi-Fi connectivity issues
  • Firmware update failures
  • Slow performance or freezing

Audio Issues

  • No sound or distorted audio
  • Speaker damage or amplifier faults
  • Bluetooth pairing problems
  • HDMI ARC audio issues

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth repairing a TV with a cracked screen?

Screen replacement can be expensive, especially for larger TVs. Our technicians will assess whether repair is more economical than replacement. For 32-43 inch TVs, screen replacement is often worthwhile. For 55+ inch TVs, we may recommend considering a new purchase depending on the model age and repair cost.

How long does TV repair take?

Most common repairs (power supply, backlight, software) can be completed within 24-48 hours. Screen replacements may take 3-5 days depending on parts availability. We keep common parts in stock for faster service.

Do you offer home visits for TV repair?

Yes, we provide on-site diagnosis and repair across Nairobi. For complex repairs requiring extensive disassembly, we may transport the TV to our workshop with your permission and return it fully repaired.

What brands of TVs do you repair?

We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Hisense, TCL, Skyworth, Synix, Vision Plus, Acer, Sony, and more. Our technicians are trained on the latest models and technologies.
