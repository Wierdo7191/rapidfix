Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

At RapidFix Kenya, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, email address, physical address
  • Location information (county, town, precise location)
  • Appliance details and repair history
  • Messages sent via contact form or email

1.2 Technical Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, time spent on site
  • Referring website URLs

1.3 Communication Information

We collect information from communications with you:

  • Messages sent via contact form
  • Email correspondence
  • Phone call details (optional recording)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Services

  • Schedule repair appointments
  • Diagnose appliance issues
  • Provide accurate quotes
  • Communicate about repair status

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Gather feedback for service improvements
  • Develop new service offerings

2.3 Marketing Communications

  • Send promotional materials (only with consent)
  • Share repair tips and maintenance offers
  • Notify about service updates and special offers

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted partners who help us deliver our services, including payment processors, logistics providers, and technical support services.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of RapidFix Kenya and our customers.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • SSL encryption for website data transmission
  • Secure storage of customer information
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Inactive customer data is securely deleted after 5 years.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to your personal data and correct any inaccurate information we hold about you.

5.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations and legitimate business interests.

5.3 Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time by contacting us at info@rapidfixkenya.co.ke.

6. Cookies

6.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your website experience and remember your preferences.

6.2 How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences (language, region)
  • To analyze website traffic and performance
  • To personalize content and advertisements

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns:
